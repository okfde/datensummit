Christian Heise

Christian Heise is political theorist / scientist and scientific assistant at Hybrid Publishing lab in the Centre for Digital Cultures at Leuphana UNiversity Lüneburg, where he does a PhD on Open Science. Previously, he was a manager at Deutsche Presse Agentur and worked at ZEIT ONLINE. Apart from his voluntary engagement in the board of OKF DE he is also founding and board member at Förderverein für Freie Netzwerke e.V. (freifunk.net). Christian likes to contribute by talking and writing on the topics Open Data, Open Government, Open Access, Open Science, and Open Networks.

Christian will welcome you at the Federal Ministry of Transport and Digital Infrastructure on 28.04.2017.
