Dorothee Bär

Since December 2013 Dorothee Bär is the Parliamentary State Secretary at the Federal Ministry of Transport and Digital Infrastructure and since January 2014 she is also the federal government’s coordinator for logistics and freight traffic. She has been a member of the federal parliament since 2002 and was the spokeswoman on family politics for the CDU/CSU’s parliamentary group.

She is a CSU-member since 1994 and was deputy Secretary General of her party from 2009-2013. Since 2010 she is the chairwoman of CSUNetzrat and since 2011 she also chairs the CSUnet. Dorothee Bär is married and has three children.
