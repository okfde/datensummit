Sylvia Frederiksson

Sylvia is designer and project coordinator at École de Données (School of Data France), and member of School of Data Internationnal Steering Committee 2016. Her work is dedicated to civil society empowerment through design, data litteracy and technology. She now works as a designer-researcher at the Cité du Design in Sainte-Etienne, France. She specialised in Hypermedia at Paris 8 University and regularly teach design classes.

Sylvia will give a lecture in the »Promoting Data Literacy« session on 28.04.2017
